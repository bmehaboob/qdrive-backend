Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

QDrive is a free, open-source QGIS plugin developed by SIFA Geolabs (sifageolabs.com) that allows users to access their Google Drive files from within the QGIS desktop application. This privacy policy explains what data the plugin accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

1. Data We Access

When you sign in, QDrive requests access to:

  • Google Drive files — to browse, download, upload, sync, rename, star, and delete files on your behalf.
  • Email address — to display your account identity in the plugin interface.

QDrive accesses only the files you interact with. We do not scan, index, or read the contents of files you do not explicitly open, download, or upload through the plugin.

2. Data Storage

  • OAuth tokens are stored locally on your computer in the QGIS plugin directory. They are never sent to any third-party server.
  • Cached files are stored locally in ~/.qdrive/cache on your computer. You can clear the cache at any time from the plugin.
  • No data is stored on our servers. The backend server only processes the OAuth authentication flow and does not store any tokens, files, or user data.

3. Data Sharing

SIFA Geolabs does not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your Google Drive data stays between you and Google. QDrive acts purely as a client-side bridge.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth 2.0 — for authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Drive API — for file operations. All API calls are made directly from your computer to Google's servers.

5. Data Retention

OAuth tokens persist until you sign out or revoke access. Cached files persist until you clear the cache or delete them manually. No usage data or analytics are collected.

6. Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • Sign out from the plugin to clear your local tokens.
  • Revoke access at Google Account Permissions.
  • Clear local cache from the plugin to delete all downloaded files.
  • Uninstall the plugin entirely to remove all local data.

7. Security

Authentication is handled via Google's OAuth 2.0 protocol. Client secrets are stored securely on our backend server and are never exposed to the plugin or end users. All communication with Google APIs uses HTTPS encryption.

8. Children's Privacy

QDrive is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
